This technology empowers farmers to plan more effectively, ensuring higher productivity and minimizing waste.

About Us

We are a team of six passionate individuals from the Informatics Institute of Technology in Bambalapitiya, currently in our second year of pursuing a degree in Software Engineering. United by our shared enthusiasm for technology and its transformative power, we embarked on a journey to make a significant impact in the field we're most passionate about: agriculture.

Driven by the belief that technology can revolutionize traditional industries, we've dedicated ourselves to developing Govisewana, a precision agriculture web application designed to empower farmers with the insights and tools they need for a more productive and sustainable future.

Our diverse skills in software development, combined with a deep commitment to leveraging technology for social good, form the cornerstone of our project. Together, we're not just a team of students; we're innovators determined to bridge the gap between agriculture and technology, aiming to create solutions that are not only effective but also accessible to farmers everywhere.

As we continue on this exciting journey, we're committed to continuous learning, growth, and the pursuit of excellence, ensuring that Govisewana remains at the forefront of agricultural innovation. Join us as we cultivate the future of farming, one digital solution at a time.
